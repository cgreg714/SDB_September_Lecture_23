Flex Practice

Accomplish

Horizontal or vertical arrangement of elements.

Space distribution of elements.

Quick Notes

Flex on a given container is one-dimensional, meaning it is either going to affect a row or a column, but not both at the same time

Useful for layouts of images galleries, navbars, etc.

Places elements that affect direct children, but not further children.

Flex Container

Applying the display of a Flex property to something, typically a "div" property. Our element with the display Flex style. This style applies to the container AND it's children.

Justify Content

Positions content along the axis of our flex container.

  1. flex-start
  2. flex-end
  3. center
  4. space-between
  5. space-around
  6. space-evenly

Align Items

Similar to justify, but works along the cross access (perpendicular to the Flex direction).

  1. flex-start
  2. flex-end
  3. center
  4. stretch
  5. baseline
